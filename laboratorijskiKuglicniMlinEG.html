HOMEPAGE    LABORATORY BALL MILL

I. LABORATORY BALL MILL

ELEMENTS

  • Mill (cylindrical construction)
  • Base
  • Motor-reducer with agitator
  • Mass recirculation pump
  • Steel balls
  • Duplex space with heater for heating water or mass
  • Container for discharging mass and balls
  • Mass cooling system
  • Process control system

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

The entire construction of the mill is made of stainless steel AISI 304, which is suitable for confectionery industry applications.

  • Working medium: chocolate (cream) mass
  • Batch mass: 10 kg
  • Final product fineness: less than 18μm

TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION

The ball mill is mainly used to produce chocolate mass and cream products. The purpose of the mill is to completely homogenize the mass and reduce the size of micro-particles of all components.

This operation is carried out using:

  • Steel balls placed inside the mill
  • Specially designed agitator
  • Automatic temperature control system using circulation of hot/cold water

The grinding process ensures complete homogenization of the mass and uniform particle size, which is essential for the quality of the final product. The temperature is continuously regulated to ensure optimal conditions for every type of mass.
