HOMEPAGE    COOLING TUNNEL

XIII. COOLING TUNNEL

ELEMENTS

  • Sturdy stainless steel construction
  • Adjustable entrance part of the tunnel
  • Cooling system with two refrigeration units
  • Air distribution channel system
  • Hoods with hinges and dampers
  • Polyurethane insulation
  • Automatic belt guiding system
  • Top cooling system—convection/radiation
  • Bottom cooling system—air/cold water
  • Belt
  • Process control system

TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION

The cooling process in the tunnel is the final phase before packaging and must be precisely controlled to ensure optimal crystallization. Efficient cooling ensures high gloss, melt resistance, prevents chocolate grayness, and results in a pleasant taste.

Our tunnels are designed for precise cooling of various chocolate masses and coatings, they are attractive in design and easy to maintain. The cooling process begins as the product leaves the enrobing machine and enters the cooling tunnel.

The entrance belt is height-adjustable, and air circulation minimally affects the area outside the tunnel. Cooling is achieved by convection, radiation, or water-cooled plates from below. Air distribution and speed are uniform, and zone temperatures are individually adjustable. Modular design enables different lengths in 2-meter sections. Automatic belt guiding and tensioning are available as options.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

  • Width: up to 2000 mm
  • Length: section 2 m (modular)
  • Material: stainless steel
