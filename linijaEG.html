Multilayer cream block line

HOMEPAGE    MULTILAYER CREAM BLOCK LINE

XIV. MULTILAYER CREAM BLOCK LINE

ELEMENTS

  • Three-part cooling tunnel
  • Chocolate mass depositor (3 pieces)
  • Cooling system via freon evaporators (Alfa Laval, Bitzer Ecostar)
  • Product cutting system
  • Electrical cabinet with equipment
  • PLC controller with tablet casting program

TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION

The process begins by pouring or dosing the cocoa tablet (the dark part of the cream block) onto the infinite belt, followed by cooling in the first part of the tunnel.

After the first layer has cooled, the second cream layer (the white part) is dosed and cooled again. Then, the third layer (again the dark layer) is dosed and cooled. This process repeats for multilayer products depending on the desired product structure.

After exiting the third segment of the cooling tunnel, the tablet reaches the cutting system, where it is cut into the desired dimensions. The size is variable, allowing for processing in various formats.

After cutting, the tablet is ready for packaging.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

  • Cream block capacity: 250 Kg/h and 500 Kg/h
  • The entire line system is made from stainless steel
