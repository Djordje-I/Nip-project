HOMEPAGE    VEGETABLE FAT MELTER

II. VEGETABLE FAT MELTER

ELEMENTS

  • Melting unit (construction)
  • Duplex space with heating system
  • Insulation
  • Process control system
  • Technical documentation with operating and maintenance instructions

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

The entire construction of the melter is made of stainless steel AISI 304, which is suitable for applications in the confectionery industry. The pipeline and associated fittings are also made of stainless steel.

  • Working medium: melting of solid vegetable fat or cocoa butter
  • Batch mass: 2000 kg

TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION

The vegetable fat melter is designed for melting solid vegetable fat or cocoa butter. After the melting process, the mass is transferred to the premixer. Melting is performed on a grid that is an integral part of the inner tank, and preheated water circulates through the grid. The process is controlled via a control panel.

This operation is performed using:

  • Grid, which is part of the inner tank
  • Preheated water circulating through the grid
  • Control panel that manages the entire process
