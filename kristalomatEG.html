Crystallomat

HOMEPAGE    CRYSTALLOMAT

VIII. CRYSTALLOMAT

ELEMENTS

  • Crystalomat (construction)
  • Duplicate space for cooling/heating
  • Rotor with cleaners
  • Crystalizer base
  • Cooling system from the customer's central cooling system
  • Heating system from the customer's central system or via heaters
  • Process control system
  • Technical documentation with operating and maintenance instructions

TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION

The Crystalomat is a device designed to shock fats. Fat shocking is a known operation in which fats are instantaneously transformed from a liquid state into small crystals and mixed with liquid fats, cooling them by dissolving.

New fats are then cooled against the walls of the Crystalomat, and thus the crystallization cycle is repeated in the Crystalomat.

The cream mass enters the Crystalomat where it is intensively mixed and cooled, and then goes to the dosing bin for cream.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

The entire Crystalomat construction is made of stainless steel AISI 304, which is specifically designed for such purposes in the confectionery industry, except for the cleaners on the rotor of the Crystalomat, which are made of Teflon.

  • Operating Medium: Chocolate (cream) mass
  • Capacity: 300 Kg/h, 600 Kg/h, 1200 Kg/h
  • Water consumption: 3 m³/h
  • Purpose: Continuous tempering of creamy masses
