IX. TURBO MIXER FOR AERATED PRODUCTS

ELEMENTS

  • Turbo mixer
  • Turbo product aeration head
  • Container base
  • Mass transport pumps – 2 pieces
  • Duplicate space with heaters and temperature maintenance system
  • Process control system
  • Technical documentation with operating and maintenance instructions

TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION

The mixer is intended to combine all components forming the aerated mass. Raw materials are introduced into the mixer via a measuring system, where specially designed mixers homogenize the mass in the shortest possible time.

The prepared mass is continuously transported via a pipeline and pump system, through the turbo head, where aeration takes place, to the depositor.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

The entire construction is made of stainless steel, which is suitable for such applications in the confectionery industry. The pipeline with fittings and the pump are also made of stainless steel.

  • Working medium: aerated mass
  • Finished product capacity: 500 kg/h
