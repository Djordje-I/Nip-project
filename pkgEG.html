V. FLOW BALL MILL

ELEMENTS

  • Base
  • Mill (cylindrical construction)
  • Drive motor with a frequency regulator
  • Belt transmission
  • Replaceable wear-resistant steel mixer
  • Shaft cooling system
  • Steel balls
  • Duplicate space with water heating and a cold water recirculation system
  • Ball discharge system
  • Mass flow meter (KROHNE)
  • Overflow tank for ball mill mass (≈50 kg)
  • Level monitoring system in the pre-mixer with a pressure sensor
  • Pipeline from the ball mill to the mass maintenance tank
  • Industrial VPN router
  • Process control system
  • Technical documentation with operating and maintenance instructions

TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION

The ball mill - homogenizer is most frequently used for producing creamy products and chocolate masses. The mill's purpose is to completely homogenize the mass and minimize the micro-particles of all components.

This operation is carried out using steel balls within the mill and a specially designed mixer.

Temperature is automatically maintained by a system that circulates cold or warm water as needed.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

The mill's internal vessel is made of stainless steel, specifically designed for confectionery purposes.

The piping are also made from stainless steel.

The load-bearing construction is made of structural steel.

Shafts and ball mixers are made from OCR materials and thermally treated.

  • Operating Medium: Chocolate (cream) mass
  • Capacity: 300 kg/h, 500 kg/h, 700 kg/h, 1000 kg/h
  • Cold water consumption: 6.5 m³/h at 10–12°C
